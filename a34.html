1. Core Rules & Fundamentals

Everything you need to memorize before tracing the algorithm.

Handling Symbols

  • A-Z

    Operands: Never push to the stack. Immediately print/add them to the Postfix output.

  • (

    Left Parenthesis: Always push directly onto the stack without comparing.

  • )

    Right Parenthesis: Pop operators from the stack to the output until you hit the matching (. Discard both parentheses.

Operator Precedence

When evaluating incoming operators against the stack's top:

Priority Operators Associativity
3 (Highest) ^ Right to Left
2 * , / Left to Right
1 (Lowest) + , - Left to Right
Same Precedence Rule: For Left-to-Right operators, if the incoming operator equals the top operator's precedence, Pop the top, then Push the incoming.
