📌 1. Pre-Requisites & Core Rules

Before inserting any nodes, the instructor explicitly states two golden rules you must constantly apply:

Rule 1: Binary Search Tree (BST) Property

Every node to the Left must be less than the parent. Every node to the Right must be greater than the parent.

Rule 2: The Balance Factor (BF) Rule

BF = Height of Left Subtree - Height of Right Subtree

  • A tree is Balanced if the BF of every node is -1, 0, or 1.
  • If ANY node hits a BF of 2 or -2, it becomes a Critical Node. You must STOP and perform a rotation immediately.

💡 2. The Instructor's "Median Shortcut" Trick

Instead of memorizing confusing LL, RR, LR, and RL rotation diagrams, the instructor uses a visual shortcut on the whiteboard. When an imbalance occurs, follow these steps:

  1. Locate the Critical Node (the one with BF = 2 or -2).
  2. Trace the path down towards the newly inserted node. Select exactly three nodes: The Critical Node, its Child on the path, and its Grandchild on the path.
  3. Out of these three numbers, find the Median (the middle value).
  4. Pull the Median UP to become the new local root.
  5. Drop the smaller number to its left, and the larger number to its right.
  6. Re-attach any "orphan" subtrees using standard BST rules.

🛠️ Phase 1: The Easy Insertions (14 to 53)

We begin with an empty tree and insert the first five elements: 14, 17, 11, 7, 53.

  • 14: Becomes root. BF = 0.
  • 17: > 14. Goes Right. BF = 0.
  • 11: < 14. Goes Left. BF = 0.
  • 7: < 14, < 11. Goes Left of 11. BF = 1.
  • 53: > 14, > 17. Goes Right of 17. BF = -1.

The instructor verbally calculates the BFs. Everything is currently between -1 and 1. The tree is balanced.

⚠️ Phase 2: First Imbalance (Inserting 4)

We insert 4. It is < 14, < 11, < 7. It goes to the Left of 7.

Audio Calculation Check:
BF of 4 = 0
BF of 7 = 1 (Left height 1 - Right 0)
BF of 11 = 2 (Left height 2 - Right 0) 👉 UNBALANCED! Node 11 is Critical.

Applying the Trick (LL Rotation):

  • Path nodes: 11 -> 7 -> 4
  • Median is 7.
  • Pull 7 up to replace 11. 4 goes left, 11 goes right.

⚠️ Phase 4: Third Imbalance (Inserting 8) - Orphan Handling!

Insert 8. It goes Left of 11.

Audio Calculation Check:
The instructor traces up from the bottom.
BF of 7 = 1 (Left path: 4) - 3 (Right path: 12->11->8) = 1 - 3 = -2. 👉 UNBALANCED! Node 7 is Critical.

Applying the Trick (RL Rotation):

  • Path nodes from Critical Node: 7 -> 12 -> 11
  • Wait, look closely at the path top! The three nodes involved in the rotation are 7, 12, 11.
  • Median is 11.
  • Pull 11 up to replace 7.
  • 7 goes left of 11. 12 goes right of 11.
  • ORPHAN ALERT: Where does node 8 go? Since 11 moved up, 8 (which was left of 11) needs a new home. By BST rules, 8 is > 7 and < 11. It becomes the Right child of 7.

🌟 Phase 6: The Final Stretch (19, 16, 20)

Insert 19 (goes Right of 17). Balanced.

Insert 16 (goes Left of 17). Balanced.

Insert 20 (goes Right of 19).

Audio Calculation Check:
BF of 53 = 3 (Left path 17->19->20) - 1 (Right path 60) = 2. 👉 UNBALANCED! Node 53 is Critical.

Applying the Trick (LR Rotation) & Orphan Handling:

  • Path nodes from Critical Node: 53 -> 17 -> 19
  • Median is 19. Pull 19 up to replace 53.
  • 17 goes left of 19. 53 goes right of 19.
  • ORPHAN ALERT 1: Node 20 was right of 19. 20 is < 53, so it becomes the Left child of 53.
  • ORPHAN ALERT 2: Node 16 remains safely attached to the left of 17.
  • ORPHAN ALERT 3: Node 60 remains safely attached to the right of 53.
